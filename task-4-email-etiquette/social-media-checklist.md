📌 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts



✅ Do’s

* Think before posting anything online
* Use privacy settings to protect your account
* Verify information before sharing
* Be respectful in comments and messages
* Maintain a positive and professional online image



❌ Don’ts

* Don’t share personal or sensitive information
* Don’t spread fake news or misinformation
* Don’t use offensive or abusive language
* Don’t post when you are angry or emotional
* Don’t copy content without giving credit
