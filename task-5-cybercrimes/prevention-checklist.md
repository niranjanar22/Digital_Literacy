**Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**



✅ **General Safety Tips**

* Verify the sender before clicking any link
* Never share passwords or OTPs with anyone
* Check website URLs carefully before logging in
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities
* Keep your device and apps updated regularly



**Financial / UPI Safety**

* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
* Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept unknown payment requests



**Reporting Channels**

* Report cybercrime at: cybercrime.gov.in
* Call helpline: 1930
